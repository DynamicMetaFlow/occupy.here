Hello!

Welcome to occupy.here, please occupy this virtual space as you wish. Occupy.here is free, anonymous, and uncensored.

You are not connected to the Internet

This website is hosted on the wifi router you're connected to. This is the only website you can access from here. Only people who are physically nearby can access this site.

What all is here?

You can watch Occupy-related videos in the video archive and you can discuss with other users in the message forum.

Who can access occupy.here?

Anyone within range of the wifi network can connect. There is no password or verification process. Messages you post in the forum will persist and may be copied to other occupy.here wifi routers.

Who works on occupy.here?

Dan Phiffer is the founder and lead developer, with further contributions from GitHub user Phaeilo. The project is all-volunteer, open source, and Free Software.

Can I have one of my own?

Yes! Occupy.here is designed to be easily replicated. Contact Dan at (314) 467-8479 if you'd like to install an occupy.here router of your own.
